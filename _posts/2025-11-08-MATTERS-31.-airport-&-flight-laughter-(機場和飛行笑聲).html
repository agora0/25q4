31. Airport & Flight Laughter (機場和飛行笑聲)

Topic: 31. Airport & Flight Laughter

Yoga Class Notes: One of 40 Foundation Exercises

Airport and Flying, Then Laugh.

We are at the airport.

We are almost late!

We carry our bags and run around the airport terminal.

Then we get our boarding passes.

I watch my bag disappear on the conveyor belt and wave hard.

Finally, I get on the plane and say goodbye!

Fake, fake!

Everything is fake!

This is our practice in the online laughter yoga class, called "Airport and Flying Laughter."

Silly? No, not silly.

After doing it, it's surprisingly fun.

Because you and I are the same.

Next is the flight safety demo. I buckle my seatbelt, then "click" and unbuckle it. Pull down the oxygen mask, put it on myself, then quickly help the air person next to me. Then, blow up the life vest. Everyone puffs their cheeks and blows hard, and don't forget to take photos.

Finally, the plane lands. Of course, it's exciting time to share photos, but the photos look like a puffy frog!

Ho ho ho ho ho….

He he he he he….

Hi hi hi hi hi….

Ho ho ho ho ho….

31. 機場和飛行笑聲

瑜珈課筆記：40個基礎練習之一

機場與飛行，然後大笑。

我們在機場。

我們快遲到了！

我們揹著包包在機場客運中心跑來跑去。

然後拿到登機證。

我看著那個消失在輸送帶上的行李用力揮手。

最後，我登上飛機說再見啦！

假的，假的！

一切都是假的！

這是我們在線上大笑瑜伽課堂上的練習，一個叫「機場與飛行大笑」的練習。

蠢嗎？不蠢，不蠢。

跟著做之後，卻意外地好玩。

因為你你我我都一樣。

接著是飛行安全示範，我扣上安全帶，又「卡」一聲地把它鬆開。拉下氧氣面罩，給自己戴上，再慌慌張張地幫旁邊的空氣人戴上，然後，吹救生衣，大家鼓起腮幫子拼命吹氣，也不忘拍照。

最後，飛機降落了，當然是讓人興奮的分享照片的時間，不過，照片卻像一隻氣鼓鼓的青蛙呢！

Ho ho ho ho ho….

He he he he he….

Ho ho ho ho ho….
