Topic: 39. Rowing Laughter

Yoga Class Notes: One of 40 Foundation Exercises

One day, the leader of the online laughter yoga class did the "rowing laughter" move. I followed the instructions, sat in front of the screen, opened my legs, and made a rowing pose. The leader said: "Come on, imagine we are rowing a boat. Make sounds with your mouth, hum-ha, hum-ha..."

I joined in right away, in just 0.0001 seconds, making the "hum-ha, hum-ha" sounds one after another.

After a few rows, the leader said: "Okay, now lean back!" I leaned back naturally. I found that I was not like before—I no longer hesitated or doubted, even though there was no boat and no other people. Laughter yoga is a great training camp. It helps me feel less shy and unnatural in front of the camera. I even thought I could be a temporary actor, like a small character in a movie by an "abstract" director.

"Ha! Ha! Ha!"

I laughed out loud, imagining I was really rowing on a lake. I looked at my home's windowsill, as if I was on a boat under the sun. The breeze and laughter moved through the room, and I felt all my muscles relax.

The happy part was, I felt I was no longer just "me," but "us"!

Rowing laughter, keep rowing.

39.划船笑

瑜珈課筆記：40個基礎練習之一

一天，線上大笑瑜珈的帶領員做（划船大笑）這個動作。我跟指示，在螢幕前坐好，雙腳打開，做出划船的姿勢。帶領員說："來，想像我們在划船，嘴巴發出聲音，哼哈、哼哈…"

我馬上用0.0001秒跟著做，此起彼落地發出（哼哈、哼哈）的聲音。

划了幾下，帶領員說："好，現在往後倒！" 我又自然地往後倒。我發現我已經不像以前，不再猶豫，不再懷疑，儘管沒有船、沒有人，因為大笑瑜珈是一個很好的訓練營，讓我對著鏡頭前都不再感到怕醜和不自然。我還覺得自己可以當個臨時演員，在一位"抽象派"導演下的一個小人物。

"哈！哈！哈！"

我放聲大笑，想像自己真的在湖上划船，看著家中的窗台，仿佛置身於船上曬太陽，微風和笑聲都在房間裡走過，感覺全身的肌肉都放鬆了。

開心的是，我感覺到我不在是（我），而是(我們)！

划船大笑，繼續划船。
